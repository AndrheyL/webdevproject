Left 4 Dead




An outbreak of a highly contagious pathogen nicknamed the "Green Flu" begins in Pennsylvania.
Four immune survivors—Green Beret and Vietnam veteran Bill, college student Zoey, district account
manager Louis, and outlaw biker Francis—leave Fairfield two weeks after the first infection, only
to learn that the sickness is causing hazardous changes in some of its hosts.








Left 4 Dead 2



Set in Savannah, Georgia, our survivors—high school football coach "Coach", gambler and con artist Nick,
production assistant Rochelle, and talkative redneck mechanic Ellis find themselves stuck at the roof of
a building after missing the helicopter, they decided to go around the city and eventually evacuate on
a bridge that will soon be destroyed by the military.
