Meet the Survivors







Bill ran out of wars after two traumatic missions in Vietnam, one of several
medals, a knee full of shrapnel, and an honorable discharge. Bill has
reclaimed the only thing he's ever wanted: an enemy to fight, after decades
of pointless drifting and dead-end jobs when the pandemic hits.

Francis acts like the zombie apocalypse is the world's biggest bar brawl,
cocky, boisterous, and very confident he's indestructible. When the virus
struck, everyone else began stockpiling supplies and looking for a safe
haven. Francis discovered a rifle and had a good time with it.

Zoey was given a choice: stop joking around and study, or drop out
spending her first semester watching zombie films. Now that the planet
has been overrun by murderous zombies and all of her professors have died,
Zoey can accept the fact that she was studying the right subject after all.

Louis had worked up the courage to resign from his job as a Junior Systems
Analyst in his company's IT department when a virus struck, shattering the
world. Hopefully, he'll figure out how the new management works before
they have a chance to kill him.




Coach has a big heart, a nutritious appetite, and a nasty chainsaw swing.
Working as a defensive coordinator for the freshmen tour team may not have
been the best path to a professional coaching career, but it has known to be
highly useful in guiding a group of Survivors to safety.

Nick has learned two valuable lessons from a lifetime of drifting from city to
city, and trying to stay out of jail: don't trust anyone, and keep an eye on
number one. Now he's thrown together with three strangers that goes against
all of his instincts. But if he wants to survive, he'll have to learn to trust them.

Rochelle's job as a low-level associate producer for a major news organization
mostly composed of hauling cables and getting coffee. But that doesn't mean
she'll give up her dream job anytime soon. Surviving a zombie apocalypse is
just another thing she can use her wits and determination to accomplish.

Ellis is a mechanic who enjoys life, thinks in his own glory, and can turn any
hurdles into a fun dare to impress his friends. The zombies then had to go and
ruin it. The zombie apocalypse turns out to be yet another big dare, and there's
no lack of crazy things he can do to impress his new friends.
