LEFT 4 DEAD


Survive the Horde!




The Left 4 Dead Series is a first-person shooter game developed and published by Valve.

Left 4 Dead is a first-person shooter in which the player takes control of one of four survivors.
You combat the Infected, individuals infected by a virus known as the Green Flu, across the game's stages.
The main aim is to make it to the next safe place alive, not to wipe out the infected.
